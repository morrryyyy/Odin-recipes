Lemon Chicken

Description

This lemon chicken recipe shines with a sunny, lemony zing. Garlic and herbs plus a little bit of white wine mean that these are juicy, tender chicken breasts are as delicious as they are healthy.

Lemon chicken is a really simple baked chicken recipe, jazzed up with some punchy ingredients that add a lot of flavour without taking away from how super-healthy baked chicken breasts are. This recipe is fast and easy and will leave the entire family wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup milk (optional)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup milk (optional)
  • 1/3 stick unsalted butter (optional)
  • 4 lemons, sliced
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F v(175 degrees C
  2. Whip milk and egg together in a small bowl. Mix flour and lemon-pepper seasoning together in another small bowl.
  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture and then in flour to coat completely. Place milk and butter in the bottom of a baking dish to keep the x=chicken nice and moist. Place coated chicken on top. Place lemon slices directly on the chicken. Cover with foil.
  4. Bake in the pre-heated oven until almost cooked through, about 30 mins. Remove foil and continue to bake until no longer pink in the centers and juices run clear, about 15 mins more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degress F (74 degrees C).
