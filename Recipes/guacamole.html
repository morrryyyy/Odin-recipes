Guacamole

Description

A simple go-to guacamole recipe that leaves you begging for more. Try it and get addicted!!

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled,pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma(plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper(Optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne, pepper.
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavour, or serve immediately.
